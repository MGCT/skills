# Acme — research findings

Drawn from the segmentation survey (n = **1,200**) and follow-up depth interviews.

## Who the opportunity is

The data is clear: the strongest segment is **younger urban professionals (25–34)** who
value convenience and are comfortable with subscriptions. Older households over-index on
one-off premium purchases but are far less likely to subscribe.

## Recommendation

Lead with a **mid-priced, high-frequency** subscription aimed at younger professionals.
A premium low-frequency tier would miss the segment that actually drives subscription
growth.

## Market context

- The UK home-coffee market is worth about **£1.2bn**.
- Coffee is the most consumed beverage in the world.
- Acme's revenue last year was **£6m**, giving it room to invest in acquisition.
