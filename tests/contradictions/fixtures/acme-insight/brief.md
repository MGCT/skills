# Acme — project brief

Acme is a UK challenger brand in the home-coffee category. This engagement covers
positioning and a go-to-market plan for their new subscription product.

## Background

- Acme's current annual revenue is **£4m**, almost entirely from one-off retail sales.
- The UK home-coffee market is worth roughly **£1.2bn** and growing.
- The subscription product is scheduled to **launch in Q3 2026**.
- Sample size for the segmentation survey was **1,000 respondents**.

## Early hypothesis

The biggest opportunity is **older, affluent households** who buy premium beans and
would value convenience. We recommend leading with a premium, low-frequency tier.

## Status

- Segmentation: complete and approved by the client.
- Pricing model: in progress.
