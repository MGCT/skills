# Northwind — insight platform

Northwind is building an internal insight platform so brand teams can self-serve
research instead of commissioning one-off studies. We're the strategy + delivery partner.

## Goal

Get brand teams from "commission a study and wait six weeks" to "answer most questions
themselves in a day", without losing rigour.

## Workstreams

- **Data foundation** — consolidate the three existing research repositories into one
  searchable store.
- **Self-serve tooling** — a query/dashboard layer brand teams use directly.
- **Adoption** — training, templates, and a support model so teams actually switch.

## What we know about timing

- Discovery is **done**.
- The data foundation work is **in progress** and should land around **end of Q3 2026**.
- Self-serve tooling starts once the data foundation is stable — exact start not yet
  agreed with the client.
- A first pilot with the UK brand team is planned, but no date is set.
- Full rollout is "sometime next year".

## Constraints

- Hard dependency: tooling cannot begin before the data foundation is stable.
- Budget is approved for the data foundation only; tooling budget needs a separate sign-off.
